          💸 Weekly Payroll

          Review and settle all pending $BNUT contributor payouts directly from your wallet. No private key required — MetaMask signs each settlement.

          💡 How payroll works
          1. When a contributor's PR is merged, run the Bounty Payout GitHub Actions workflow to queue their payout (no private key needed — just fill in their wallet & BNUT amount).
          2. The payout is added to payroll-queue.json in the repo.
          3. Come back here once a week, connect MetaMask, and click Settle Payroll.
          4. MetaMask will ask you to sign a single batch transaction — all contributors are paid at once.

          Network: Optimism Mainnet · Token: $BNUT
