          About the Project

          BigNuten helps you track the combined effects of regular exercise, vitamins, and supplements on your body over time. Log workouts, weight, nutrition, and emotions — all in one place, stored locally and optionally on IPFS for decentralized persistence.

          Technologies

          • 🌐 Vanilla HTML / CSS / JavaScript
          • 📦 IPFS (InterPlanetary File System)
          • 🦊 MetaMask & Ethereum wallet integration
          • 📊 Chart.js for data visualization
          • 🌙 Moon & Sun astronomical tracking

          Roadmap

          • ✅ Local data logging (weight, supplements, food, exercise)
          • ✅ IPFS snapshot persistence
          • ✅ Emotion wheel tracking
          • 🔜 Crypto subscription via Decent contract
          • 🔜 AI-powered nutrition recommendations
          • 🔜 Social sharing & community challenges
