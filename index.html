            📡 Your Data & IPFS Storage

            BigNuten stores your health data locally and backs it up on IPFS — a public, peer-to-peer file network. Choose how your snapshots are stored.

            ⚠️ Community Data Notice: BigNuten is a community health experiment. Your data is not encrypted and is stored on IPFS — a public network. Only share what you're comfortable with others potentially seeing. 📚 Learn about IPFS →
            Active: ☁️ DecentAgency Storage
            ☁️
            Use DecentAgency's Space Default
            Zero setup — we handle IPFS for you

            Your data is automatically pinned to the BigNuten project's IPFS space. No account needed. The project team can see your snapshot CIDs (but not your private keys or wallet data). Great for getting started quickly.

            ⚠️ Visibility: When using DecentAgency's space, the project owner can view the IPFS CIDs of your snapshots, and your data is on public IPFS. Do not store sensitive health data you wouldn't want others to see.

            💸 Weekly Payroll

            Review and settle all pending $BNUT contributor payouts directly from your wallet. No private key required — MetaMask signs each settlement.

            💡 How payroll works
            1. All entries in payroll-queue.json are loaded as pending payouts — the queue is the authoritative source.
            2. Each pending payout is cross-checked on-chain via isIssuePaid(); already-paid issues are automatically excluded.
            3. The Batch Preview below groups payouts by wallet, tallies the total BNUT owed, and lists all covered issues per contributor.
            4. Connect MetaMask and click Settle All Pending to send a single batch transaction. Wallets without a registered address are skipped with a warning.
            5. After settlement, the Recently Settled section is populated live from ContributorPaid events on-chain.
