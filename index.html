              Settings

              🌅 Day Cycle Start / End Time

              Set when your personal day begins. All stats (water, steps, exercise, supplements, etc.) reset at this time. Useful for shift workers or early risers.
              Default: 04:30 AM. Community leaderboards always use UTC midnight.

              💡 Examples: 00:00 = midnight  ·  04:30 = 4:30 AM (default)  ·  18:00 = 6 PM (third-shift workers)

              🌈 Chakra Aura Mode

              Animates each chakra on the BigNuten body figure based on your health data. Glow intensity, breathing speed, and a body-aura canvas overlay all reflect how active each energy centre is today.

              💡 Disable to stop all chakra glow effects and the aura canvas overlay. Your data is unaffected — you can re-enable at any time.

              💡 How payroll works
              1. All entries in payroll-queue.json are loaded as pending payouts — the queue is the authoritative source.
              2. Each pending payout is cross-checked on-chain via isIssuePaid(); already-paid issues are automatically excluded.
              3. The Batch Preview below groups payouts by wallet, tallies the total BNUT owed, and lists all covered issues per contributor.
              4. Connect MetaMask and click Settle All Pending to send a single batch transaction. Wallets without a registered address are skipped with a warning.
              5. After settlement, the Recently Settled section is populated live from ContributorPaid events on-chain.
